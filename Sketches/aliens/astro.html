Poem

Astro has a two-lobed brain. Each side argues with the other, Driving Astro insane. Astro needs a helmet To contain his thoughts. His brainstorms register A thousand watts. Astro's world Is an expansive space, An eternal exterior, And lots of head space. Astro has the idea before the thought. Yes, he is an Astro not.
