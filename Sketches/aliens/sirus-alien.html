Poem

The animals think Sirus Alien’s son, is God’s avatar, But Sirus Alien’s son sees the animals As expensive caviar. Aliens cause trouble wherever they are. Sirus Alien had a solitary son, Who went by the name of Orion. He was a cosmic drifter, An entrepreneur, a genetic shapeshifter. His dad called him Son, He called his dad Mister. Yes Orion had a dad, it’s not so bizarre, Then had a kid, Dad passed out cigars, Then packed his son off To a faraway star.
