Poem

Orion, the Original Alien. This is the history, this is the lesson. Orion descended From the far side of heaven, With his pet Rabbits, numbered eleven. His taser beam created the hole Where animals all dare to go In search of their immortal souls— Where all shall be delusional. One day all the animals Who believe the delusion Will rise with Orion To the foregone conclusion, And render real The grand illusion.
